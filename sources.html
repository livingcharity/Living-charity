Sources & References

Transparency, integrity, and responsibility are the foundation of this project

Purpose of This Page

Living Charity is built on the belief that goodness must be rooted in truth. This page exists to clearly declare the moral, spiritual, and human sources that inspire this project, while maintaining honesty, openness, and respect for all people.

Primary Moral & Spiritual Sources

  • The Holy Qur’an — as a source of guidance, justice, mercy, and belief in one God, without distortion or selective interpretation.
  • The Sunnah of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ — as a model of compassion, integrity, humility, and service to humanity.

These sources are referenced with respect, accuracy, and scholarly responsibility. No content is added or altered to serve personal or political agendas.

Universal Human Values

  • Compassion and mercy
  • Justice and fairness
  • Honesty and trustworthiness
  • Respect for human dignity
  • Peaceful coexistence

These values are shared across cultures, religions, and civilizations, and form common ground for global cooperation.

Ethics of Artificial Intelligence

This project aligns with internationally recognized AI ethics principles, including but not limited to:

  • Transparency and explainability
  • Human-centered design
  • Non-harm and safety
  • Accountability and responsibility
  • Respect for human values and beliefs

Commitment to Accuracy

We commit to:

  • No misinformation or fabricated sources
  • No forced ideology or manipulation
  • Clear distinction between facts, beliefs, and opinions
  • Correction of errors when identified

Open Review & Community Oversight

Living Charity is an open project. Scholars, developers, ethicists, and community members are welcome to review, suggest improvements, and flag inaccuracies through the public repository.

Living Charity is a non-profit, open initiative dedicated to goodness, knowledge, and ethical responsibility.
