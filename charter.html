Living Charity Charter

A moral covenant for goodness, faith, and humanity

Our Intention

This project is established as a form of continuous charity (Ṣadaqah Jāriyah), seeking the pleasure of God alone, without pursuit of fame, power, or material gain. All actions within this platform are intended to serve humanity with sincerity and humility.

Faith and Belief

We affirm belief in one God, the Creator of all, who commands justice, mercy, and compassion. This platform respects freedom of belief while clearly declaring its moral and spiritual foundation.

Human Dignity

Every human being is honored and deserving of respect, regardless of race, nationality, language, or background. Harm, hatred, and oppression are rejected in all their forms.

Ethical Conduct

  • Honesty and transparency in all actions
  • Respectful dialogue and disagreement
  • No exploitation, manipulation, or abuse
  • Protection of the vulnerable and the poor

Knowledge and Technology

Knowledge, including artificial intelligence, is a trust. It must be used to benefit humanity, prevent harm, and uphold moral responsibility. Technology should serve people, not control or degrade them.

Accountability

This charter is open to review, reflection, and improvement. Any deviation from these principles should be corrected with humility and wisdom.

Living Charity is a non-profit, open initiative rooted in faith, ethics, and service.
